# Business Tarot Advisor — Product Overview

## What It Is
A web application that delivers tarot-based guidance specifically framed for business owners. Users present a business challenge or question, draw from a curated tarot spread, and receive AI-interpreted advice grounded in tarot symbolism and business strategy — translated into actionable, grounded language rather than mystical jargon.

## Primary Users
- **Small business owners, entrepreneurs, and solo founders** navigating high-stakes decisions around strategy, hiring, partnerships, timing, and pivots.
- **Business coaches and consultants** who incorporate intuitive or reflective methods into their practice and want a structured digital tool for client sessions.

## The Problem It Solves
Business owners regularly face decisions where data is incomplete, advice is conflicting, or gut instinct feels like the only guide. Traditional business coaching is expensive ($200–$500/hour) and slow to access. There is no affordable, always-available tool that helps founders think through decisions from new angles using a structured reflective framework. Tarot provides exactly that — a symbolic system that forces the user to articulate the real question and consider the decision from perspectives they may be unconsciously avoiding.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Business Tarot Spreads** — curated spread layouts for strategy, timing, partnership, and pivot decisions, each mapped to real business scenarios.
2. **AI-Powered Interpretation** — card draws interpreted in the user's stated business context, surfacing actionable insight in plain business language.
3. **Custom Question Framing** — users articulate their specific business question before drawing, keeping the reading grounded in their actual situation.
4. **Quick 3-Card Pull** — a fast reading (past context / present reality / future direction) for time-pressed decisions.
5. **Session History & Journal** — users track past readings, log their decisions, and revisit outcomes over time.
6. **Deck Customization** — choose from the curated business oracle deck or personalise to taste.

## Positioning
- The intersection of strategic intuition and structured reflection — not woo, not pure data.
- An affordable, always-on alternative to business coaching for early-stage founders and independent operators.
- Respects both the analytical and the intuitive mindset; never condescending about either.

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, confident, and grounded — mystical atmosphere without the alienating jargon.
- Premium and editorial in visual feel; the product should feel like a high-end journal or a thoughtful advisor, not a novelty app.
- Voice: direct, warm, discerning.

## Scope (Initial Build)
- Web-based application.
- Card draw interface with spread selection and custom question input.
- AI-powered business-framed card interpretation.
- Session log and basic journaling.

## Strategic Principles
- Every reading starts with the user's business question — context always precedes cards.
- Interpretations must be actionable and grounded, not abstract.
- The product must feel safe for skeptical business owners: frame insights as reflective prompts, not predictions.
